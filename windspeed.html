Wind Speed



This visualization compares the wind speed (in miles per hour) to the latitude of the individual city.

There does not appear to be a strong correlation between these two factors. Across latitudes, wind speed is mostly clustered at less than 10mph. Variance in these measurements may suggest 2 things:

  • As noted in the humidity analysis, winter is probably more stormy closer to the north pole.
  • Or is it? It could also relate to the theory posited in the max temperature section, this could also be due to the shape of landmasses at the extremes. It is possible that having weather measurements from the southern oceans could change that perception.
